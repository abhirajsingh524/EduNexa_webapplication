About EduNexa

Welcome to EduNexa

EduNexa is an AI-powered e-learning school platform that helps students learn smarter through personalized learning paths, smart assessments, and performance analytics.

Teachers can manage courses and track progress, parents can monitor learning reports, and students receive recommendations to improve weak areas step-by-step.
